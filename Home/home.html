Our History

Established in 2003, Mario Group has been a cornerstone of gaming culture in Lebanon. From our humble beginnings as a small arcade to becoming a beloved destination for gaming enthusiasts, our journey has been one fueled by passion and dedication.


Over the years, we've witnessed the evolution of gaming technology, yet our commitment to providing an exceptional arcade experience remains unwavering. Our rich history is a testament to our love for gaming and our patrons.


Today, Mario Groupe stands as a testament to our commitment to preserving the magic of arcade gaming while embracing the innovations that drive the industry forward.

Our Mission

At Mario Group, our mission is simple: to be the ultimate destination for gaming enthusiasts of all ages. We strive to create an environment that fosters joy, camaraderie, and unforgettable gaming experiences.


We are dedicated to curating a diverse collection of games that cater to the varied tastes and preferences of our patrons. Whether you're seeking the nostalgia of classic arcade games or the thrill of cutting-edge technology, our arcade is your playground.


Our commitment extends beyond entertainment. We aim to create a community hub where gamers can connect, compete, and celebrate their passion for gaming through events, tournaments, and shared experiences.

Community Engagement

At Mario Group, we believe in giving back to the gaming community. That's why we regularly host events, tournaments, and themed nights that bring together gamers from all walks of life.


From charity fundraisers to competitive gaming leagues, we are committed to fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our patrons. We value the relationships we build and cherish the support of our vibrant gaming community.


Join us in our mission to celebrate gaming, friendship, and shared experiences. Together, let's create lasting memories and continue to elevate the world of arcade gaming!

Since 2003, Mario Groupe has been the source for authentic arcade fun. We sell thousands of arcades all over Lebanon, making us the largest arcade & kit distributor.
